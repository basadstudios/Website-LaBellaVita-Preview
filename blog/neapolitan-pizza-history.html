The History of Neapolitan Pizza

April 22, 2023
Chef Marco Colombo
pizzafood historyitalian cuisine
When you bite into an authentic Neapolitan pizza, you're not just enjoying a delicious meal – you're taking part in a tradition that spans centuries. As the pizzaiolo at La Bella Vita, I'm passionate about honoring this tradition while bringing it to our guests in Chicago. ## Origins in Naples Pizza as we know it today evolved in Naples, Italy, during the 18th and 19th centuries. What began as flatbread with toppings became a distinctive culinary art form. The working-class people of Naples needed affordable, quick food that could be eaten without utensils – and pizza was the perfect solution. ## The Margherita Legend In 1889, pizzaiolo Raffaele Esposito created a pizza for Queen Margherita of Savoy using tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil – ingredients representing the colors of the Italian flag. This "Pizza Margherita" became legendary and remains the benchmark for Neapolitan pizza. ## What Makes Neapolitan Pizza Special Authentic Neapolitan pizza follows strict guidelines: - Dough made only with flour, water, salt, and yeast - Hand-kneaded (no mechanical processing) - Baked in a wood-fired dome oven at extremely high temperatures (850-900°F) - Cooked for just 60-90 seconds - Soft, elastic center with charred, puffy edges (cornicione) At La Bella Vita, we import Italian 00 flour and San Marzano tomatoes, make fresh mozzarella in-house, and maintain our sourdough starter with the same care as a newborn child! ## Protected Status True Neapolitan pizza is so important to Italian culture that it received protected status from the European Union and an official certification process through the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana (AVPN). Next time you join us, watch through our open kitchen as we stretch the dough by hand and transform simple ingredients into something magical in just 90 seconds of intense heat. It's a culinary technique that has remained largely unchanged for generations – and we're proud to bring that authentic experience to you.
